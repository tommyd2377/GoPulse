Welcome to GoPulse

GoPulse is the next generation of social news. It was designed to prevent the spread of misinformation and to be completely free of advertising. With GoPulse you can:

  • Get a much broader perspective on current events
  • Discover, read and share the latest news from a growing list of vetted publishers
  • Follow other GoPulse users to get article recomendations designed to keep you informed and open minded
